Banana Pancakes

Description

These banana pancakes are a staple at my house. My kids and my wife love them. These are completely customizable and can be made to your own tastes. I will just give a blueprint on how to start.

The basic ingredients of the pancake are bananas, eggs, oatmeal, and some flour. I typically will use 3 very ripe bananas. The more ripe the banana is, the sweeter the pancakes will be. I tend to make sure the bananas are just beginning to brown, not completely brown.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of cinammon
  • few drops of vanilla (optional)
  • chocolate chips or sprinkles (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mash the bananas in a bowl.
  2. Step 2: Mix in eggs and oats and combine.
  3. Step 3: add in pinch of salt and cinnamon to taste.
  4. Step 4: Get frying pan ready over medium heat with a little oil covering bottom of pan.
  5. Step 5: Add a spoon full of batter to frying pan. Depending on size of pan, you might be able to fit a few at a time.
  6. Step 6: At this part you can add your chocolate chips or sprinkles to the pancakes on the pan.
  7. Step 7: Be ready to flip the pancake when you notice little bubble around the edges.
  8. Step 8: Flip the pancake and wait for the other side to finish, about 1-2 more minutes.
  9. Step 9: Take them off the pan and repeat with the remaining batter. Eat with more sliced bananas and maple syrup. Enjoy!
